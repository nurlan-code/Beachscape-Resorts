Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our resort or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Payment information (credit card details, billing address)
  • Travel preferences and special requests
  • Identification documents for check-in purposes
  • Dietary restrictions and health information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Note: We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services and improve your experience with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use your personal information to:

  • Process reservations and bookings
  • Provide customer service and support
  • Manage your stay and preferences
  • Process payments and billing
  • Send important updates about your reservation

2.2 Marketing and Communications

With your consent, we may use your information to:

  • Send promotional offers and special deals
  • Share resort news and updates
  • Invite you to participate in surveys
  • Send birthday and anniversary greetings

2.3 Legal and Safety

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Respond to law enforcement requests
  • Enforce our terms and conditions

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing and billing
  • Email and communication services
  • Customer relationship management
  • Data analytics and website optimization
  • Travel and activity booking services

3.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the new entity as part of the transaction.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our guests or others.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against:

  • Unauthorized access and use
  • Accidental loss or destruction
  • Malicious attacks and breaches
  • Improper disclosure or alteration

4.2 Data Encryption

All sensitive information is encrypted during transmission and storage using industry-standard encryption protocols.

4.3 Access Controls

Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job functions.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information. You can do this by contacting us directly or through your online account.

5.2 Data Portability

You have the right to receive a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format.

5.3 Deletion Rights

You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal and operational requirements.

5.4 Marketing Opt-out

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Cookie Usage

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Improve website functionality and performance

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

7. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
