Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms and conditions carefully before making a reservation at Beachscape Resorts. By booking with us, you agree to these terms.

1. Reservation Terms

1.1 Booking Confirmation

All reservations are subject to availability and confirmation. A valid credit card is required to secure your booking. You will receive a confirmation email with your reservation details upon successful booking.

1.2 Payment Terms

Payment terms vary by room type and season:

  • Standard rooms: Full payment required at time of booking
  • Suites and villas: 50% deposit required, balance due 30 days before arrival
  • Peak season bookings: Full payment required 60 days before arrival

Important: All prices are quoted in USD and include applicable taxes and service charges unless otherwise specified.

2. Cancellation Policy

2.1 Standard Cancellation

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival date. Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival will be subject to a one-night room charge.

2.2 Peak Season Cancellation

During peak season (December 15 - January 15, July 1 - August 31), cancellations must be made at least 7 days before arrival to avoid charges. Cancellations within 7 days will incur a 100% charge.

2.3 No-Show Policy

Guests who fail to arrive on their scheduled check-in date without prior notice will be charged the full amount of their reservation.

3. Check-in and Check-out

3.1 Check-in Times

Check-in time is 3:00 PM. Early check-in requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. We will do our best to accommodate early arrivals when possible.

3.2 Check-out Times

Check-out time is 12:00 PM. Late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Late check-out after 6:00 PM will be charged at the full nightly rate.

3.3 Identification Requirements

All guests must present valid government-issued photo identification upon check-in. International guests must present a valid passport.

4. Resort Policies

4.1 Age Requirements

Guests must be at least 18 years old to make a reservation. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by an adult guardian.

4.2 Smoking Policy

Beachscape Resorts is a non-smoking property. Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas, including guest rooms, restaurants, and public spaces. Designated smoking areas are available outdoors.

4.3 Pet Policy

Pets are not allowed on the resort property, with the exception of certified service animals. Service animals must be registered with the front desk upon arrival.

4.4 Dress Code

Resort casual attire is appropriate throughout the property. Formal dining venues may require smart casual or formal attire. Swimwear is restricted to pool and beach areas only.
