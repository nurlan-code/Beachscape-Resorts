Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our resort, services, and policies to help plan your perfect tropical getaway.

What is included in the room rate?

Our room rates include daily housekeeping, WiFi, access to resort facilities, beach equipment, and concierge services. Some premium amenities may have additional charges.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we provide complimentary airport transfers for all guests. Our luxury vehicles will pick you up and drop you off at the airport for a seamless arrival experience.

What activities are available?

We offer a wide range of activities including water sports, spa treatments, golf, cultural tours, cooking classes, and exclusive island experiences. Our concierge can arrange any activity.

Is there a cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. Please check your specific booking terms for detailed cancellation conditions.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Our restaurants can accommodate all dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please inform us of your requirements when booking.

What is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit is during the dry season from November to April when you'll enjoy sunny weather, calm seas, and perfect conditions for all outdoor activities.
